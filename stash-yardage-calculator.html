About the Stash Tracker

Keep a running inventory of your yarn stash right in your browser. All data is stored locally — no account needed.

  • Add as many yarns as you like — name, weight, yardage, and color notes.
  • The summary shows total yardage broken down by weight category.
  • Use the search bar to find a specific yarn, or filter by weight to plan a project.
  • Export your stash as a text file for backup or sharing.

Data is stored in your browser's localStorage. Clearing browser data will erase your stash — use Export regularly.
