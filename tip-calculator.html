How to use

  • Enter your total bill amount — results update instantly as you type.
  • Select a preset tip percentage or choose Custom to enter your own.
  • Enter the number of people splitting the bill.
  • Optionally enter a pre-tax amount to calculate tip on food only (before tax).
  • Check "Round up" to round each person's share to the nearest dollar.
  • Use "Copy Result" to share the breakdown with your group.
