How Etsy Fees Work (2024)

  • Listing Fee ($0.20) — Charged each time you list or renew an item. Auto-renews every 4 months or when sold.
  • Transaction Fee (6.5%) — Applied to the full sale price including shipping you charge the buyer.
  • Payment Processing (3% + $0.25) — For Etsy Payments (required in most countries).
  • Offsite Ads (12–15%) — Only charged when a buyer clicks an Etsy ad outside the platform and then purchases.

Tips to Maximize Your Payout

  • Factor in all fees when setting your prices — don't price based on item price alone.
  • Charge accurate shipping — transaction fees apply to shipping, so free shipping isn't always worth it.
  • If you're under $10k/yr, consider opting out of Offsite Ads to save 12% on those sales.
