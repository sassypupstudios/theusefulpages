How to use

  • Use "Hourly → Salary" to see your annual equivalent from an hourly rate.
  • Use "Salary → Hourly" to break down your salary into an effective hourly rate.
  • Adjust hours per week and weeks per year to match your actual work schedule.
  • Tax estimates are rough US federal only — state taxes and deductions not included.
  • Use the overtime calculator to see your weekly pay with overtime hours.
