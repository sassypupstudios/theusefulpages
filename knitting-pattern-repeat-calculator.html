How to Use This Calculator

  • Enter your total stitch count (from your cast-on calculator or pattern).
  • Enter the pattern repeat size — e.g. "6" for a 6-stitch repeat.
  • If the pattern says "multiple of 8 plus 3," enter 8 for repeat size and 3 for extra stitches.
  • Selvedge stitches are edge stitches that frame the pattern — common in flat knitting. Enter 1 for a 1-stitch selvedge on each side.

Understanding "Multiple of X plus Y"

Many patterns describe stitch multiples like "multiple of 6 plus 2." This means the repeat is 6 stitches, plus 2 extra stitches at the end to balance the pattern visually. Example: 38 stitches = 6 × 6 repeats + 2 extra = valid for "multiple of 6 plus 2."

Written Instruction Format

The calculator generates a written instruction like knitting patterns use, showing the breakdown of selvedge + repeats + extra stitches.

Results are estimates. Gauge, yarn, and needle type affect actual results.
