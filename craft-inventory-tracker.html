How to use this tracker

Add every supply item or finished product to your inventory. Set a reorder point — when quantity on hand drops to or below that number, the row is flagged red so you know to restock. Filter by "Low stock only" to build a shopping list quickly.

  • Adjust qty directly in the table as you use supplies or sell items
  • Cost per unit × qty = total inventory value, shown in the summary
  • Export to CSV to keep records in a spreadsheet

Data is stored in your browser's localStorage and does not sync across devices or browsers.
