How to Use the Pillow Cover Size Calculator

Select your pillow insert size from the dropdown or enter a custom size. Choose your preferred seam allowance. The calculator will show cutting dimensions for three closure types:

  • Envelope Closure: Two overlapping back pieces — no zipper needed. Quick and easy to remove for washing.
  • Zippered Cover: Clean finish with an invisible zipper. Front and back panels cut to the same size with zipper allowance built in.
  • Simple Sewn: Two identical panels sewn together. Stuff the insert then hand-stitch or slip-stitch to close.
