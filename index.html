Frequently Asked Questions

Are these tools free?

Yes. The tools are free to use and do not require a paid account.

Do I need to sign up?

No. Open a tool and use it right away without creating an account.

Is my data uploaded?

Most tools run in your browser, so the text or numbers you enter stay on your device unless a tool clearly says otherwise.

Does it work on mobile?

Yes. The site is designed to be usable on phones, tablets, laptops, and desktops.
