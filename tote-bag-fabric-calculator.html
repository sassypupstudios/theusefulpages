How to Use the Tote Bag Fabric Calculator

Choose a quick preset or enter custom dimensions. Select your seam allowance and strap size. The calculator builds a complete cutting list for outer fabric, lining, and straps, then gives you total yardage to buy.

Panel Overview

  • Front & Back panels: The main body of the bag (2 pieces).
  • Bottom strip (gusset): Only needed when you enter a depth > 0. Creates a box base.
  • Straps: Cut 2 pieces. Fold in thirds or use interfacing for structure.
  • Lining: Usually the same dimensions as the outer shell minus seam allowances for ease.
