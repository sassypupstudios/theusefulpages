How to Use the Project Cost Calculator

  1. Add each material (yarn, notions, buttons, etc.) with quantity and unit cost.
  2. Enter how many hours you spent and your desired hourly rate (use minimum wage, your craft rate, or $0 for personal projects).
  3. Add an overhead percentage to cover electricity, packaging, or general expenses (optional).
  4. Your totals update live. Use "Export as Text" to save a copy.

Your data is saved automatically in your browser.
