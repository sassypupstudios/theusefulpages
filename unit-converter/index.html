How to use

  • Select a category tab (Length, Weight, Temperature, etc.)
  • Enter a value in either the From or To field — results update instantly
  • Use the dropdowns to change the units on each side
  • Click the ⇄ button to swap the two units
  • Use quick-convert buttons for the most common conversions
  • The formula used is displayed below the converter
  • Click Copy Result to copy the converted value to your clipboard
