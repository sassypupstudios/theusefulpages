How to use

  • Give your pattern a title, paste the written pattern text, then click Build Checklist.
  • The tool detects labels like Row 1:, Round 3:, Rnd 2:, R4: and splits automatically.
  • If no labels are found, each non-empty line becomes its own checklist item.
  • Click any checklist row to mark it done (or undo it).
  • Progress is saved automatically to your browser. Save up to 10 patterns.
  • Switch between saved patterns using the chips at the top.
  • Use Export as Text to copy your pattern with checkmarks.
