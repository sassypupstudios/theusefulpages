About this tracker

Your project list is saved in your browser's localStorage — it stays between visits on the same device and browser. No account or internet connection needed to use this tool.

  • Add notes about the pattern source, thread colors, fabric count, or your progress notes
  • Update % complete as you stitch — click Edit on any project
  • Filter by status to focus on active projects or your finished collection

Data is stored locally in your browser. Clearing browser data or switching devices will remove your project list.
