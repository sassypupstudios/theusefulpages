How to use this calculator

If you know the finished size you want (e.g. to fit a specific frame), enter the dimensions and select your fabric count. The calculator tells you how many stitches wide and tall your design needs to be to fill that area. Useful when designing from scratch or scaling an existing pattern.

  • Design a custom piece to fit an 8" × 10" frame on 14-count Aida = 112 × 140 stitches
  • Compare how different fabric counts change the stitch count for the same design area
  • Plan pixel-art designs at a target size

These are the stitches needed to fill the area. Your actual pattern may be smaller — that is fine. The calculation shows the maximum stitch count that will fit in that area.
