How to Use This Estimator

  • Select a standard size or enter your chest circumference directly.
  • Adjust body length, sleeve length, and yarn weight.
  • Click Estimate Yardage to see a breakdown by section and total.

Tips

  • The estimate covers a standard adult sweater with 1–2" ease. Add 10–15% for textured stitches.
  • Buy all skeins from the same dye lot — colors can vary slightly between lots.
  • Cardigans typically use 5–10% less yarn than pullovers due to the front opening.
  • Use the reference table above to cross-check your estimate.
