How to use

  • Enter the total amount you plan to borrow in the Loan Amount field.
  • Input your annual interest rate (APR) as a percentage.
  • Set the loan term in years or switch to months for shorter loans.
  • Click Calculate to see your monthly payment and full amortization schedule.
  • Toggle "Show All Months" to view every payment period.
  • Use "Copy Summary" to copy the results to your clipboard.
