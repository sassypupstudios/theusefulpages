About Difficulty Levels

Beginner (0–3): Simple stitches, straight construction, no shaping. Perfect for first projects.
Easy (4–6): Basic shaping or a few colors. Straightforward but requires some attention.
Intermediate (7–9): Multiple techniques, in-the-round construction, charts, or moderate shaping.
Advanced (10–12): Complex construction, special stitches, multiple simultaneous techniques.
Expert (13–15): All the hard stuff at once — charts, multiple colors, intricate shaping, seaming, and more.

This is an estimate based on common difficulty factors. Your personal experience level will also affect how challenging you find a pattern.
