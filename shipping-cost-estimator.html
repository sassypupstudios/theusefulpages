How to estimate shipping for craft orders

Weigh your packaged item on a kitchen or postal scale. Enter the weight and choose the package type and destination zone. This tool gives you estimated rate ranges to use in your pricing — always verify exact rates on USPS.com or your carrier's site before charging customers.

  • Under 13 oz: USPS First-Class is usually cheapest; Ground Advantage for tracked shipping
  • Heavier items: compare Ground Advantage vs Priority Flat Rate — sometimes flat rate wins
  • Always include your packaging cost (box, tissue, labels, tape) in the shipping charge
  • Offer free shipping by building the average cost into your item price

Estimates are based on approximate 2024 USPS retail rates and are for planning purposes only. Actual rates vary by exact weight, dimensions, and carrier. Always use carrier tools for final rates.
