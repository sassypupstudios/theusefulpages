How to Use This Calculator

  • Select a size preset or enter custom measurements.
  • Enter your gauge in stitches per inch — sock yarn is usually knit at 7–9 sts/inch.
  • Click Calculate to get your cast-on count, heel flap stitches, and yardage per sock and per pair.

Tips

  • Most sock patterns are knit with negative ease — the foot circumference of the sock should be about ½" less than the actual foot circumference.
  • A standard 100g fingering weight skein (400–450 yards) is usually enough for one average women's sock. Two skeins = one pair.
  • The heel flap stitch count is typically half the total cast-on stitches.
  • Gauge swatch in the round for the most accurate result, as gauge often differs between flat and circular knitting.
