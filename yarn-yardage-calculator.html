How to Use the Yarn Yardage Calculator

  • Select a project type preset or choose "Custom" and enter your own dimensions.
  • Enter the width and length of your finished project in inches or cm.
  • Select your yarn weight — this determines how many yards of yarn are used per stitch.
  • Enter your gauge (stitches per inch). Check your yarn label or work a swatch.
  • Click Calculate Yardage to see how many yards and meters you need.

Tips

  • The "Yards + 20% Buffer" result is what we recommend buying to avoid running out mid-project.
  • Lace and textured stitch patterns often use more yarn than stockinette for the same area.
  • Yarn weight defaults (yards per stitch) are averages — heavier weights use more yarn per stitch.
  • If you already have yarn, divide total yards by yardage per skein to find how many skeins you need.
