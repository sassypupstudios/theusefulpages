How to Find Projects for Leftover Yarn

Have a partial skein and not sure what to make? This tool matches your yardage to a list of common projects by weight category.

  • Projects marked as a perfect fit are well within your yardage.
  • Projects marked as a close fit may work but leave very little yarn — or require a tight gauge.
  • Yardage requirements vary by needle/hook size, gauge, and pattern. These are typical ranges.
  • Mix-and-match colors! Many of these small projects are great for using up multiple partial skeins.

Yardage shown is an approximate range. Always check your specific pattern for exact requirements.
