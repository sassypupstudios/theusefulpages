How to use

  • Choose Semester GPA to calculate GPA for a single term, or Cumulative GPA to combine previous and new semesters.
  • For Semester GPA: enter each course name (optional), its credit hours, and your letter grade. Your GPA updates in real time.
  • Click + Add Course to add more courses and the remove button (×) to delete a row.
  • For Cumulative GPA: enter your existing GPA and credits, then your new semester's GPA and credits to see your updated cumulative GPA.
  • Class standing is based on total completed credit hours: Freshman (0–29), Sophomore (30–59), Junior (60–89), Senior (90+).
  • Most schools use the standard 4.0 scale shown in the grade table. A+ and A both equal 4.0 points.
