How to use

  • Enter an event name and target date/time, then click Start Countdown.
  • Set a start date for the progress bar to show how much time has elapsed.
  • The display turns yellow under 1 hour remaining and red under 10 minutes.
  • Enable Sound on completion to hear a beep when the timer reaches zero.
  • Click Save to store the event in your browser across visits.
  • Click Share to copy a URL with the target date pre-loaded.
