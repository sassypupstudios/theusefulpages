How to use

  • Use Savings Growth to project how much a deposit + contributions will grow over time.
  • Use Goal Calculator to find the monthly contribution needed to hit a target amount.
  • Switch to "Find Years Needed" in goal mode to see how long it takes at a given monthly amount.
  • Interest is compounded monthly in all calculations.
  • The inflation note shows your future balance in today's purchasing power (at 2.5% inflation).
