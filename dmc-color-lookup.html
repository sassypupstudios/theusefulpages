DMC produces over 500 embroidery floss colors. This table includes 80+ of the most popular. Search by number (e.g. "321") or by name (e.g. "coral"). Filter by color family using the dropdown.

Saving Favorites

Click the star (☆) next to any color to save it as a favorite. Favorites are stored in your browser and persist between visits. Click "★ Favorites" to show only your saved colors.

Color Families

Use the color family filter to quickly browse a category. This is especially helpful when you need to find a specific shade of red, blue, or brown for your project.

About the Swatches

The color swatches shown are CSS approximations of the actual DMC floss colors. They are meant as a rough guide only — always refer to a physical DMC color card or the actual thread for accurate color matching. Colors vary significantly by monitor calibration and lot number.
