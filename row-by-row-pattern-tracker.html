Row-by-Row Pattern Tracker

Track your current row or round for up to 5 projects. Big tap targets, progress bar, and notes — all saved automatically.

Maximum of 5 projects reached.

How to use

  • Click + Add Project and give it a name (e.g. "Granny Square Blanket").
  • Set the total rows/rounds so the progress bar knows your goal.
  • Tap the large + button to advance to the next row, or − to go back.
  • Add optional notes — yarn color changes, stitch notes, reminders.
  • Mark a project Done when it's finished — the card turns green.
  • All data is saved automatically in your browser. Up to 5 projects at once.
  • Use Export All as Text to save a snapshot of all your projects.
