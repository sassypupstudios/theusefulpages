How to Estimate Yardage from Grams

Every yarn weight category has a typical yards-per-gram (yd/g) ratio. By weighing your yarn and knowing the category, you can estimate how much yardage you have — perfect for mystery skeins, inherited yarn stashes, or unlabeled balls.

  • Use a kitchen scale to weigh your yarn in grams.
  • Identify the yarn weight by wrapping it around a ruler and counting wraps per inch (WPI).
  • The result is a range — use the lower end for planning to be safe.

These are estimates. Actual yardage may vary. For precision, wind and measure a length of yarn with a niddy noddy or yarn swift.
