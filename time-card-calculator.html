How to use

  • Each day is enabled by default — uncheck the box next to a day to disable it (e.g. weekends off).
  • Enter clock-in and clock-out times for each active day. Use standard time pickers or type manually.
  • Enter break duration in minutes (e.g. 30 for a half-hour lunch). This is subtracted from your hours.
  • Optionally enter your hourly rate to see your regular pay, overtime pay, and total estimated pay.
  • Overtime is calculated after 40 hours by default (standard US). Change the threshold if your employer uses a different cutoff.
  • Use Quick Import to paste times in bulk: one line per day starting Monday, format 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM, 30.
  • Click Save to store your current time card in your browser. Click Load Saved to restore it.
