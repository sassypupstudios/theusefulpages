How to use

  • Click + New Project to add a project with name, type, status, yarn, hook size, gauge, and notes.
  • Click Edit on any card to update details. Click Delete to remove it.
  • Use the search bar to find projects by name, yarn, notes, or any field.
  • Filter by status (Planning, In Progress, Frogged, Finished) using the filter buttons.
  • All data is saved automatically in your browser — nothing is sent anywhere.
  • Click Export All to download a plain-text summary of all your projects.
