How to Use

  • Enter all your costs: booth fee, travel, setup supplies, display materials.
  • Enter the total material cost you spent making the inventory you're bringing.
  • Enter your expected or actual sales to see profit and margin.
  • Use the break-even unit calculator to know how many items you need to sell at each price point.

Craft Fair Tips

  • A good rule of thumb: aim to make 3–5× your booth fee in sales.
  • Track actual vs. expected sales to know which shows are worth returning to.
  • Consider your time as a cost — even if you don't enter it here.
  • Keep unsold inventory for the next show rather than discounting on the day.
