Hooks vs. Needles — What Sizes Overlap?

Crochet hooks and knitting needles share the same metric (mm) shaft diameter system, so a 5mm hook and a 5mm (US8) needle are the same diameter. However, US numbering differs between the two tools — a "G/6" crochet hook is 4mm, while a "US6" knitting needle is also 4mm (they match!).

  • UK/Canadian numbers are different for hooks vs. needles — always verify by mm.
  • Steel crochet hooks use a separate numbering system not shown in the main table.
