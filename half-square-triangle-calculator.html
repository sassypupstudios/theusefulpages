How to Use the HST Calculator

  • Enter the finished size of your HST — the size it will be after sewing into your quilt block (not including seam allowance).
  • Enter how many HSTs you need total for your project.
  • Click Calculate to see cutting sizes for both methods and how many squares to cut.

2-at-a-Time Method

  • Layer two squares right sides together.
  • Draw a diagonal line from corner to corner.
  • Sew ¼" on each side of the drawn line.
  • Cut on the drawn line. Press open. Trim to unfinished size.
  • Each pair of squares yields 2 HSTs.

4-at-a-Time Method

  • Layer two squares right sides together.
  • Draw both diagonal lines (X pattern).
  • Sew ¼" on each side of both drawn lines.
  • Cut on both diagonal lines. Press open. Trim to unfinished size.
  • Each pair of squares yields 4 HSTs (but they will be slightly less accurate than the 2-at-a-time method).

Formula Reference

  • 2-at-a-time cutting size = Finished size + 0.875"
  • 4-at-a-time cutting size = Finished size + 1.25"
  • Unfinished HST size = Finished size + 0.5"
