About the floss tracker

Keep a running inventory of your embroidery thread collection. Add colors as you buy them, mark what you've used up, and keep a wish list of colors you want. The search bar lets you quickly look up any color number or name.

  • Use "Need to Buy" to build a shopping list before your next craft store run
  • Export to CSV to open in Excel or Google Sheets for printing
  • Works with any brand — DMC, Anchor, Sulky, Madeira, or custom

All data is stored in your browser's localStorage. Clearing browser storage will erase your inventory.
