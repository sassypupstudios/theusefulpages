For informational purposes only — not medical advice. Due dates are estimates — only about 5% of babies are born on their exact due date. Always follow your healthcare provider's guidance.

How to use

  • LMP method: Enter the first day of your last menstrual period — adds 280 days (40 weeks) adjusted for cycle length.
  • Conception method: Enter your approximate conception date — adds 266 days (38 weeks).
  • Ultrasound method: Enter the date of your scan and how many weeks/days pregnant you were at the time.
  • The timeline shows key milestones with dates — passed milestones are highlighted.
  • Your current pregnancy week and trimester update automatically.
  • Due dates are estimates — normal delivery ranges from 37–42 weeks.
