How to Use the Temperature Blanket Planner

  1. Choose °F or °C for your temperature unit.
  2. Add up to 15 color ranges — enter the min temp, max temp, and color name for each range. The colors do not need to overlap.
  3. Each day, enter the date and that day's high (or low, or average — pick one and stick with it!) temperature.
  4. The tool will match the temperature to a color range and add it to your log.
  5. Your data is saved automatically in your browser. Use "Export Text" to copy your log.

All data is stored locally in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server.
