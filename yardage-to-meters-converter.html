Yards to Meters Conversion

The exact conversion factor is: 1 yard = 0.9144 meters (defined exactly).

  • To convert yards to meters: multiply by 0.9144
  • To convert meters to yards: multiply by 1.09361

This is useful when a pattern uses metric measurements but your yarn label is in yards (common with US yarns vs. European patterns), or vice versa.
