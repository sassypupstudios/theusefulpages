How to Use

  1. Paste or type your text into the box — stats update instantly.
  2. Select a reading speed: Slow (150 wpm), Average (200 wpm), Fast (250 wpm), or enter a custom speed.
  3. Reading time and speaking time update automatically as you change the speed or the text.

Speaking time is calculated at 125 words per minute — the typical pace for a comfortable presentation or speech. Use it to plan talks, podcasts, or voiceovers.

Average adult reading speed is 200–250 wpm for non-fiction and 150–200 wpm for technical content.
