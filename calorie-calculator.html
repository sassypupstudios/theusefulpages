How to use

  • Select Imperial or Metric units, then enter your sex, age, height, and weight.
  • Choose your activity level — be honest for the most accurate results.
  • Set your goal: Lose, Maintain, or Gain weight.
  • BMR is your basal metabolic rate — calories burned at complete rest.
  • TDEE is your total daily energy expenditure including activity.
  • Weight loss target uses a 500 cal/day deficit (~1 lb/week); gain uses +300 cal/day surplus.
  • Macros are split 30% protein / 40% carbs / 30% fat (standard balanced split).
