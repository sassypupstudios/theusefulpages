How to use

  • Select the type of QR code: URL, Text, Email, Phone, SMS, or WiFi.
  • Fill in the required fields — the QR code updates automatically as you type.
  • Choose a size (128, 256, or 512 px) and error correction level.
  • Optionally customize foreground and background colors.
  • Click Download PNG to save the QR code image.
  • Higher error correction (H) makes QR codes scannable even if partially damaged.

About this Qr Generator

Use this free QR code generator to turn text, URLs, or short information into a downloadable QR code. It is useful for flyers, labels, signs, business cards, menus, events, and quick sharing. No signup is required, and the tool works on phones, tablets, and desktop browsers.
