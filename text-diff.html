How to Use

  1. Paste your original text in the left panel and your modified text in the right panel.
  2. Optionally enable Ignore case or Ignore whitespace for a looser comparison.
  3. Click Compare to see the diff result.
  4. Green lines are additions, red lines are deletions, and plain lines are unchanged.

About this Text Diff

Use this free text diff checker to compare two pieces of text side by side and spot differences. It is useful for drafts, edited copy, code snippets, notes, contracts, and version checks. No signup is required, and the tool works on phones, tablets, and desktop browsers.
