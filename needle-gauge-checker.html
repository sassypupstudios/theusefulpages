How to Use

  • Identify My Needle: Use a needle gauge card (the metal or plastic card with holes) to measure your needle diameter in mm, then enter it above.
  • Gauge-Check: Count stitches over 4 inches in your swatch, enter that number, and find the typical needle size for that gauge.
  • Conversion Table: Browse the full mm ↔ US ↔ UK reference below.

How to Measure Your Needle

  1. Insert the needle into the round holes on your needle gauge card.
  2. Find the smallest hole the needle fits through without forcing — that is your needle size.
  3. Read the mm measurement printed beside that hole.
