Time to Make Calculator

Find out how many days it will take to make a target number of items given your available daily work hours.

About this calculator

Use this tool to plan your production schedule before a craft fair, holiday rush, or wholesale order. Time how long one item takes to make, then enter your available daily hours and this tool tells you exactly when you'll finish — or how many items you can realistically complete by a deadline.

  • Time yourself making one item first for the most accurate result
  • Build in buffer time — don't schedule every available minute
  • Use this alongside the Batch Production Calculator to account for batch efficiency

Finish dates are calculated as calendar days, skipping non-work days based on your days-per-week setting.
