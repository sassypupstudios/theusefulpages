Scrabble Word Finder

Enter up to 7 tiles (use ? for blank/wildcard). Find your highest-scoring plays.

How to use

  • Type your tile letters into the boxes — one letter per box.
  • Use ? for a blank tile (worth 0 points, can be any letter).
  • Use the filters to narrow results by length, starting/ending letters, or a required letter.
  • Results are sorted by Scrabble score by default — highest scoring first.
  • The Top 10 section shows your best plays with letter-by-letter scores.
  • Orange highlighted tiles indicate blank tile usage (0 pts).
  • Word list loads once and is cached for 7 days for fast results.
