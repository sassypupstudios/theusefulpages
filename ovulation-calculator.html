How to use

  • Enter the first day of your most recent period.
  • Set your average cycle length — 28 days is the average but many women range from 21–35 days.
  • Enter how many days your period typically lasts.
  • Click Calculate to see your ovulation date and fertile window.
  • Ovulation typically occurs 14 days before your next period starts.
  • The fertile window is 5 days before ovulation plus ovulation day itself — sperm can survive up to 5 days.
  • The 3-month chart shows upcoming cycles, fertile windows, and ovulation days.
