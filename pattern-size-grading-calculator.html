How to Use the Pattern Grading Calculator

  • Enter the measurements listed on the pattern envelope or instructions (the "pattern size") in the middle column.
  • Enter your own body measurements in the right column.
  • Shoulder width and back length are optional but help with more accurate grading.
  • Click Calculate Grading to see the difference at each point and how much to add or remove.

Understanding the Results

  • Total Diff — How many inches larger (+) or smaller (−) your measurement is compared to the pattern.
  • Per Side — For circumference measurements (bust/waist/hip), the adjustment is spread across the sides of the pattern. Divide by 4 for a standard front-and-back 2-piece pattern (each seam gets ¼ of the adjustment).
  • For length measurements (back length), the total difference is added or removed at a single point.

Important Limitations

  • This calculator gives a simple linear estimate. True pattern grading curves seam lines and shifts design elements proportionally.
  • Always make a test garment (muslin or toile) before cutting expensive fabric.
  • For differences larger than 2 sizes (4"), consider buying the nearest size and doing a full adjustment rather than grading.
