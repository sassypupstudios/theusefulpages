How to use

  • Choose Imperial (feet/inches, pounds) or Metric (cm, kg).
  • Enter your height and weight, then click Calculate BMI.
  • The gauge shows where your BMI falls on the WHO scale.
  • Your healthy weight range is calculated from BMI 18.5–24.9 for your height.
  • BMI = weight (kg) ÷ height (m)². For imperial: BMI = 703 × lbs ÷ inches².
  • BMI is a population screening tool — it does not account for muscle mass or body composition.
