How to Use the Curtain Fabric Calculator

Enter your window measurements and preferences. The calculator handles all the math for fullness, hems, and heading allowances.

Key Terms

  • Window Width: Measure your rod from end to end, not just the window glass.
  • Drop: Distance from rod to where curtain should end (floor, sill, or apron).
  • Fullness Factor: How gathered/full the curtains will look. 2× is standard for most fabrics.
  • Heading: The top treatment. Rod pocket and tab-top need extra fabric for the pocket/tabs.
