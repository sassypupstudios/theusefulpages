How to Use the Gather Ratio Calculator

Finished → Cut Length: Enter the finished (gathered) measurement you need — for example, to gather a skirt waistband that is 30" around, enter 30. The calculator tells you how long to cut the fabric strip before gathering.

Cut Length → Finished: If you already have a fabric strip cut, enter that length and ratio to find what finished gathered length you'll get.

Gather Ratio Guide

RatioFullness LevelBest ForNotes
1.5:1LightHeavier fabrics, subtle effectCut 1.5× the finished length
2:1StandardMost fabrics, skirts, sleevesCut 2× the finished length
2.5:1FullRuffles, light cotton, voileCut 2.5× the finished length
3:1Very FullRuffles on light fabricsCut 3× the finished length

Important: Heavier fabrics (denim, canvas, velvet) need a higher ratio for the same visual fullness because they don't compress as easily. Lightweight fabrics like chiffon or lawn achieve excellent fullness at 1.5:1 or 2:1.
