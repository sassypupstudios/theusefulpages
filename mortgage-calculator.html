How to use

  • Enter the home price and your down payment (as dollars or a percentage).
  • Select your loan term (15, 20, or 30 years) and enter your interest rate.
  • Add annual property tax and homeowner's insurance estimates.
  • If your down payment is less than 20%, enter your PMI rate (typically 0.5–1.5%).
  • Click Calculate to see your full PITI monthly payment breakdown.
  • Review the year-by-year table to see how your balance decreases over time.
