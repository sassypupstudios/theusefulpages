How to Use This Calculator

Ring → Size

  • Enter the number of stitches in your magic ring (commonly 6 or 8).
  • Select your yarn weight and enter your hook size in mm.
  • Enter how many increase rounds you plan to work (the increases add 6 stitches per round for a 6-stitch ring).
  • Click Estimate Size to see the approximate finished diameter.

Size → Ring

  • Enter your desired finished diameter in inches.
  • Select yarn weight.
  • Click Find Ring Count for the recommended starting ring size and number of rounds.
