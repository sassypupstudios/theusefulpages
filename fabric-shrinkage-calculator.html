How to Use the Fabric Shrinkage Calculator

Pre-wash → Post-wash mode: Enter the dimensions of your fabric before washing and select the fabric type. The calculator will estimate how much it will shrink, giving you the expected dimensions after washing.

Reverse mode: If you know the finished size you need, enter that size and the calculator will tell you how much fabric to buy before washing to ensure you end up with enough.

Shrinkage Rates by Fabric Type

FabricShrinkage RangeNotes
Cotton3–5%Most common; shrinks more on first wash
Linen3–4%Pre-washing recommended
Wool5–10%Use the average unless felting is desired
Polyester<1%Very stable; minimal shrinkage
Silk2–3%Dry clean or cold hand wash
Rayon5–10%Can be significant; always pre-wash
Bamboo3–5%Similar to cotton

Tip: The calculator uses the average of the shrinkage range. Wash temperature, drying method, and weave tightness all affect actual shrinkage.
