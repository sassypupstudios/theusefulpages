How to Use

  • Click "+ New Product" to start a fresh cost sheet for a new product.
  • Add each material with its quantity and cost per unit.
  • Enter packaging, labor hours, hourly rate, and any marketplace fees.
  • Click "Save Product" to store it in your browser for future visits.
  • Use "Export as Text" to copy a summary to paste into notes or emails.

Pricing Tips

  • 2× markup is often used for wholesale. 2.5–3× is common for retail.
  • Include marketplace fees so your markup reflects true profit.
  • Update costs whenever material prices change.
