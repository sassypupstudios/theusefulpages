How to Use This Calculator

  • Select a standard blanket size or choose "Custom" and enter your own dimensions.
  • Enter your crochet gauge — work a 4"×4" swatch and count stitches and rows.
  • Select your yarn weight for a yardage estimate.
  • Click Calculate to see your chain count, row count, and yardage.

Tips

  • Add extra stitches to your foundation chain for turning chains (usually 1–3 depending on stitch height).
  • Gauge varies significantly by stitch pattern — always work a swatch in the actual stitch you plan to use.
  • For blankets with borders, reduce your main stitch area slightly to account for border width.
