Filet Crochet Foundation Chain Formula

Standard dc mesh: foundation chain = (grid squares × 3) + 5

  • Each mesh square = ch 2, 1 dc (3 sts per square)
  • Add 5 for the turning chain setup at the start
  • First row: dc in 8th ch from hook, then repeat (ch 2, skip 2, dc) across
