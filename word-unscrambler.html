Word Unscrambler

Enter scrambled letters to find every word you can make. Click any word to see its definition.

How to use

  • Type your scrambled letters (up to 15) in the input box.
  • Choose minimum word length and sort preference.
  • Click Unscramble to find all possible words.
  • Click any word chip to look up its dictionary definition.
  • The number next to each word is its Scrabble point value.
  • Use Copy All Words to copy every result to clipboard.
  • The word list loads once and is cached for 7 days for fast results.
